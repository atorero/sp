Business meets Science

We are living in a great epoch of an ongoing technical revolution. The world around us is changing rapidly: every day we see new devices that facilitate our life. Smartphones that can in most cases replace computers, robot vacuum cleaners, electric and even self-driving cars! We can continue this list forever. A lot of research is actively going on and many solutions are implemented as real things.

We need a huge amount of scientific investigations. However, it is often tricky for entrepreneurs to perform research. Laboratories cost a lot of money. People must be trained for many years before they can produce any scientifically meaningful results. Most of ongoing research will not actually produce any positive outcome, since researchers only have some hypothesis and are never sure they will actually find something (of course, negative outcome is also valuable, but normally cannot be used in business). Many products could be significantly improved if a larger amount of research is involved.

On the other side, researchers have their own difficulties: they have to search for the funding of their projects. They have to understand what are real needs for the society. It is very difficult for them to find a niche for their products, perform proper licensing, launch the products into production and discover appropriate marketing techniques. That explains why most of discoveries, ideas, patents just stay unimplemented and not visible to others.

We have started our project to facilitate the two counterparts - Business and Science - to meet each other and fruitfully work together.
